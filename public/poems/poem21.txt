One Less Person
I’m worried. Panicked. Out of it. 
My heart’s beating fast
Drumming with no rhythm
As questions fly around my head

Why is there always something
That fucks with my goddamn brain
Something always pulling me down
My tears are lost in the rain

I want to give up
I want it to be over
But I can’t. 
I just can’t.

Because that’s one less person 
Who makes others smile
One less person
Who fights for what’s right
One less person 
To plug the holes of a sinking ship
One less person
Who will be genuine and kind by default

But I need help
There’s too many people 
Who will prioritize greed and power
Over respect kindness and compassion 

There’s too many people
Who are so comfortable being horrible
There’s too many people
Who think their actions have no consequence
There’s too many people
Who live selfishly 100% of the time

And it’s incredible to me
That people worship prehistoric ideals 
A concept designed to bring communities together
Has done nothing but tear us apart

Not enough people 
Have independent thoughts
Not enough people
Understand the damage that’s being caused

I want to fight
But have no tools but my brain
No help from the common good
Not enough standing up for what’s right

Still, I will fight
I will work until the day I die
Putting smiles on faces
Of people who won’t understand my impact
Until I’m gone.
