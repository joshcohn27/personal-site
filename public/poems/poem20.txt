Out of Options
The happiness bursting out from the start
Has fizzled quicker than a sugar rush
I’m trying to keep going and push every day
But anxiety and stress take over 

The horror of living each day
Of going through motions
Doing things I dislike
Over and over

Food has no taste 
Appetite disappeared 
Anxiety attacks daily
Not a way to live

But what else can I do
I want to live
I like being me
I like so much about my life

But the difficulties weigh heavier
The challenges. The painful moments
The looming future
The frustrating moments

It’s hard to keep going
To keep growing 
To keep getting better
To keep living 

But there’s no other option
The hardships define us
Our resilience is tested 
And our growth defines success
