The Ride
dedication: Dedicated to my Grandpa Larry Levin
I didn’t realize
you were my role model
until I began to see myself
in the things you taught me

not in words
but in the way you moved through the world

in patience
when things were difficult
in compassion
when people needed it 

in humor
that made everyone feel lighter
in empathy
that made people feel seen

the older I get
the more I recognize the resemblance

not just in our lives
but in the quiet choices that shape them
in the morals you stuck to
in the standards you set

you lived a long 
and good life
filled with joy 
and love

the kind of life
people remember
because they felt better
having known you

that kind of impact
is a rare gift
one you carried with you
each and every day

one you passed down
without ever saying so

I see it now
in the way people respond to me
and I promise
I will keep using it
the way you did

I hope I live a life
that would make you proud
and when my work here is done

I will see you again.
