Prism
I’m lost, though I know where I stand
My brain spinning like a tornado
Pieces of me swirling in the air
Trying to find mine

Delicate. Confused. Misunderstood
Echoes of shouting through the walls
Fights that never ended
Love that never stayed

Searching for balance
For something that doesn’t tilt beneath me
Like a flame in the windBending.
Fighting to stay lit

Unsure of my feelings
New thoughts, or maybe old ones
Unsure of my future
But I will find someone

Someone who sees me clearly
Someone to gather my debris
Someone who shields the fire 
Someone I don’t have to earn
