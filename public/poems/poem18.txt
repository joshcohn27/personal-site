Blurred or Broken?
Emotions I can’t quite place
Conflicting thoughts pinballing around my head
How do you take it one at a time
when you don’t even know where to start

Grappling with my reality
Trying to separate
what I think
from what I do
and which one is actually me

Losing sight of myself
but still showing up
like that counts for something

Breathing but not alive
Going through the motions
Going through us
like muscle memory
without the feeling

I keep reaching for something clear
but everything blurs
before I can hold it

A month is not a long time
and somehow it drags
like I’ve been here forever

Some of the nerves
from having none at all
like silence where something loud
used to live

But I should be excited, right?
I should keep moving?
Keep pushing along?
That’s what they tell me

But lies blend with the truth
Emotions blend with actions
and I can’t tell if I’m healing,
or just getting better at pretending.
